Hotel La Bodo

Full Stack Project

Details

  • Date : February 2021

  • Category:Full Stack

About the Project

I acted as a project lead for a group of 5 students in order to create a hotel website that catered to the COVID-19 situation that happened in 2019 where every Singaporean had to be quarantined and extreme precautionary measures had to be taken. Our website mainly caters to the front-line workers that previously had a hard time due to being exposed to the virus in copius amounts, thus not allowing them to return back home so as to not risk infecting their family members. These workers are forced to sleep over at the various hospitals they are working at, and our group strives to solve that problem by allowing them to rest their body and mind luxuriously and satisfyingly inside the premises of a 5-star hotel.


I managed to facilitate the many discussions that took place during the 3 months that we had used to create the website. I assisted in solving problems that they had regarding the technological side like code errors or advanced features.

My Part

I was responsible for the Guest Booking System that made use of the other components of the website such as a user system, and additional COVID-19 items supplied to the hotel. Whenever a guest signs up in the website and wants to book a room, the system will automatically extract the relevant details about the guest like their name and place of stay, and insert them into a booking form.


I was also responsible for allowing the system adminstrators/admin-side to create a brand new hospital, occupation, industry, or room service request entry. They are also able to delete, update, and view these entries in detail.


In particular, I was able to incorporate Google's API into the hospital function in the form of being able to use their Google Maps API feature to pinpoint the exact location and coordinates of the hospital they want to create an entry for. Afterwards, I allowed the system to automatically extract the data about the hospital directly from Google's database and fill them into another form, for convenience and security purposes.
